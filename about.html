Our Mission

Make it simple—and transparent—to save on IIN. We surface the best working code, explain where it applies, and show you exactly how to redeem it at checkout.

What We Do

  • Track & verify IIN discount codes, promo codes, and official sitewide offers.
  • Publish clear guides to flagship programs like HCTP and advanced options like CIP, plus popular short courses.
  • Explain eligibility (new student, exclusions, stackability) so you know what to expect.

How We Verify Codes

  1. Checkout tests: We test on a flagship program and at least two short courses; “working” means the order total drops.
  2. Evidence: We keep screenshots and note the last verified time in Asia/Taipei.
  3. Labels you can trust: Working / Limited / Not working / Expired—no vague “maybe”.

Reminder: IIN promotions are typically non-stackable; the checkout page is the source of truth.

Editorial Standards & Disclosure

  • Independence: We are not affiliated with IIN.
  • Accuracy: We avoid exaggerated claims and mark limits clearly (course exclusions, region, student status).
  • Transparency: Some outbound links are sponsored/affiliate (rel="sponsored"); this does not change your price.
  • Privacy: We keep tracking minimal and only to improve user experience.

Who We Serve

  • New students enrolling in IIN’s Health Coach Training Program (HCTP).
  • Coaches advancing skills through CIP or topic-specific short courses.
  • Busy learners who want a verified code fast and clear next steps.
